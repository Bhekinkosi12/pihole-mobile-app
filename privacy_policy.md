# Privacy Policy

`Pi ContrHOLE` listed on the App Store by developer `Parade, Inc (MA)` does not collect any personal information.  

Pi ContrHOLE distributed through the App Store may communicate with Microsoft App Center for to track crash and analytics data.  App Center anonymizes all data - you can learn more about [App Center here](https://appcenter.ms/).  If you'd like to disable this functionality, you may always build this app from source.

Pi ContrHOLE does not communicate anything back to the developer, and only communicates with the single IP address the user explicitly specifies.
